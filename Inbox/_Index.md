# Inbox & Meetings Index

Use Inbox for raw capture before classification.

## Capture Rules
- Do not overthink capture. Put rough material here first.
- During processing, convert captures into concepts, research, decisions, projects, meetings, or experiments.
- Every processed note should link to at least one MOC/project/concept.

## Processing Checklist
- What type of note is this?
- Does it belong to a project/domain?
- What should it link to?
- Does it imply a decision, experiment, or next action?
- Should it be archived after processing?
